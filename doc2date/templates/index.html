doc2date

About

doc2date is a prediction engine that tries to guess the publication date of a document. It is trained on works of fiction over the period 1700-1922 and works best on text segments from such works.

doc2date is open source. To learn more about how doc2date was created, take a look at the github repository.

To learn more, you can contact me (Benjamin Wallace) at me [at] bcwallace [dot] com or visit my website.
